Opportunities

In Hilversum there are several language schools and recruiting agencies that can offer opportunities for refugees. On this page you can find out about this organizations and quick links to their websites.

LANGUAGE SCHOOL

Danner & Danner

Danner & Danner offers courses for people who want to do their integration exams as well as for people who want to improve their Dutch level. The municipality of Hilversum is paying for lessons, however, note that it might take a while before you get a course here. As the demand for free language courses are high it can take several months before you are called for an intake. If you want to start right away at Danner & Danner, you can pay for the course yourself. Here you can also make use of your DUO loan for integration.

LANGUAGE SCHOOL

Fairfield College

Fairfield College has courses for people who want to upscale their Dutch level into professional level. Here you can do your integration course too. The school offers group lessons, but you can take private lessons if that better suits with your needs. It can take up to two weeks to be invited for an intake after you apply. Here you need to use your own funds to pay or DUO loan.

Looking for work?

Agency

Refugee Talent Hub

Refugee Talent Hub brings employers and refugees together with paid employment as main goal. (2023 Refugee Talent Hub) This agency gives you opportunities to connect with potential employers who want to help you out. All you need is to make an online account in the website. Make sure to upload your updated cv or resume and specify your field of interests. Give out as much information as needed in order to succeed. In the Events webpage of the website you can have a look on upcoming events where you get the chance to meet with different organisations representatives. Make sure to keep an eye on events and show up if you register.

Agency

RefugeeWork

RefugeeWork is the national platform that brings employers and refugees together through skill-based matching. RefugeeWork facilitates the search for the right vacancy or candidate and takes care of the first contact, after which the follow-up proceeds through the companies’ standard procedures. RefugeeWork also provides clear information in the refugee’s language regarding regulations, grant opportunities, and special issues to consider. RefugeeWork is an initiative by VluchtelingenWerk Nederland and Start Foundation. source
