Help with integration

Everyone who have intentions to live in the Netherlands is obligated to integrate. In this page you can find point of contacts in case you have questions about or while your integration process.

Governmental

Hilversum Sociaal Plein

Municipal department of social affairs is your first point of contact in any case. Every new resident gets connected to a contact person. Reach out to your contact person with any question about integration and living in Hilversum. Schedule meetings with them to plan your integration journey.

Find out answers of common topics at www.hilversum.nl

Non-Profit organisation

VluchtelingenWerk

Dutch council for refugees (VWN) can be reached for various questions that you might have during your integration. They can help you to connect with a free language coach for individual practice. VWN can help you to fill governmental forms, submit tax return etc. They offer some free courses and workshops for people in specified interests and goals.

Address: Orchideestraat 8, 1214 BJ Hilversum

Non-Profit organisation

UAF

The UAF is founded specially for refugee students and professionals who asked for or received asylum in the Netherlands. They collaborate with education institutions, companies and municipalities. You can get coaching and financial support from UAF to start or complete your studies. There are several conditions to meet for assistance.
