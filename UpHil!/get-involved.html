Get involved

When you are new in the city it might be hard to find events, communities where you can meet new people and build your connections network. In this page you can find some useful information and tips about things to do in Hilversum and find your way around.

Bibliotheek Hilversum

Language café

Bibliotheek Hilversum (the library) offers language café for everyone with any language level. These gatherings are held regularly in the week with different groups. Facilitators are volunteers and they do their best to help everyone. You can practice speaking, listening, reading, and writing.

To register for language café you need to visit the library’s help desk on Monday from 12.30 - 14.15 where you’ll discuss your level and the group where you want to participate. There is no participation fee.

Address: 's-Gravelandseweg 55, 1217 EH Hilversum

Hilversum

Friend4Friend

Friend4friend is a project of the Maatschappelijke Diensttijd (MDT), aimed to connect young Dutch people and newcomers. After an intake interview you will be paired with a Dutch buddy with who you will be doing volunteer work in Hilversum. The volunteer work can be decided by the couple based on their interests. The project takes 6-8 months with group sessions every month. At the end of the project, you will receive a certificate, which can be useful for your portfolio.

Facebook group

HelloEverybody Hilversum!

There are online communities to join. One of the many groups is HelloEverybody Hilversum! on Facebook. Founded in 2013, this group is aimed to help Dutch and Expats to find new people with commonalities and make friends in Hilversum. They host events, gatherings, workshops, language café and much more.
